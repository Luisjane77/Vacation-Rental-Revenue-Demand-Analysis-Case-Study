# Revenue Management Implications

This section connects the analytical findings of the case study with business and revenue-related interpretation.

The objective is not to treat the project as a pure pricing model, but to show how descriptive analysis, segmentation insights, and predictive modeling can contribute to stronger commercial judgment in the vacation rental sector.

The analytical outputs included in this case study can support thinking around demand behavior, customer value differences, rental performance, and revenue-related decision-making. In that sense, this section represents the bridge between technical analysis and practical business use.

## Main Focus Areas

- Demand-related interpretation
- Customer value and behavioral differences
- Pricing-related implications
- Commercial performance understanding
- Translation of analysis into business judgment
